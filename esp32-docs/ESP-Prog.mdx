## Traditional ESP32 vs. Built-in JTAG (S3 / C-Series)

- Standard ESP32: Requires an external JTAG adapter (like an ESP-Prog) connected to specific JTAG pins: TDI, TDO, TCK, TMS, and GND.
- ESP32-S3 / ESP32-C3 / ESP32-C6: These chips feature a built-in USB-to-JTAG converter. You only need to wire your board's GPIO 19 (D+) and GPIO 18 (D-) directly to a computer USB port. You do not need additional hardware like an ESP-Prog or FTDI board.
